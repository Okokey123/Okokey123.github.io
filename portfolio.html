Our 1.3 Scratch Project! Presenting.... Flappy Bird!


We had used the coding platform in order to create a Flappy Bird remake. In the actual game, there are three different speeds; normal ( slow ), fast ( advanced ), and extreme. Each one incrementally faster than the last. The goal is to get through all of the pipes without hitting them, attempting to reach the highest score possible. With a variety of different costumes, different backgrounds, animation, sounds, and more we had fullfilled 7 of the requirements for this project.
